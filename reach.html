How to Reach

Our holy place is located in Gujarat. Here are the directions:

By Air:

The nearest airport is located in the city of Ahmedabad. From there, you can hire a taxi or use public transport to reach the holy place, which is approximately 120 kilometers away.

By Train:

The nearest railway station is Mehasana Junction. From the station, you can take a taxi, bus, or other local transportation to reach the holy place, which is approximately 50 kilometers away .

By Road:

The holy place is well-connected by roads. You can reach there by private car, bus, or hired taxi from nearby cities.
